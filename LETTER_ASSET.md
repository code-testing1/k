## Recommended Styles:

**Best options:**

1. **Lined notebook paper** - Classic ruled paper with blue/red lines
2. **Vintage letter paper** - Aged/brown paper with subtle texture
3. **Plain cream paper** - Simple textured paper background
4. **Old parchment** - Vintage brown-toned paper

**Avoid:**

- Very dark papers (text won't be readable)
- Highly decorative papers (distracting from text)
- Papers with existing writing
